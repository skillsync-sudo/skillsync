Community Guidelines

Building a professional Nepal, together.

Our mission is to foster a safe, productive, and professional environment for skill-sharing and career growth in Nepal.

1. Professional Respect

Treat every community member with dignity. We have zero tolerance for:

  • Harassment, bullying, or hate speech.
  • Discrimination based on caste, religion, gender, or geography.
  • Defamatory comments that violate the National Penal (Code) Act, 2074.

2. Authentic & Truthful Participation

  • Real Identity: Use your real name and accurate professional details.
  • No Misinformation: Do not post fake job listings or misleading course reviews.
  • Spam: Avoid repetitive posting or unauthorized commercial advertisements.

3. Privacy & Safety

Respect the "Right to Privacy." Never share another user’s phone number, address, or private documents without explicit permission.

4. Professional Purpose

Skillsync is a platform for internships, jobs, and networking. Please keep discussions focused on professional development. Non-professional content (political campaigning, adult content, or personal grievances) should be avoided.

5. Intellectual Integrity

Only share resumes, portfolios, and articles that you own. Plagiarism or claiming someone else’s work as your own will lead to immediate account review.

Reporting & Enforcement

If you see something that violates these rules, please report it immediately:

  • Email: skillsync@gmail.com
  • Action: Consequences range from a formal warning to permanent IP bans and, if necessary, reporting to the Nepal Police Cyber Bureau.
