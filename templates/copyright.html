Copyright Policy

Compliant with Nepal Copyright Act, 2059

At Skillsync, we respect the intellectual property rights of others. This policy outlines our procedures for addressing claims of copyright infringement in accordance with Nepal’s Copyright Act, 2059 (2002) and international digital standards.

1. Reporting Infringement

If you believe your copyrighted work is being used on Skillsync without authorization, please submit a formal notice to our Copyright Agent including the details listed below.

2. Notice Requirements

  • Signature: A physical or electronic signature of the copyright owner or authorized representative.
  • Identification: Clear description of the work claimed to have been infringed.
  • Location: The specific URL or location on Skillsync where the material is found.
  • Contact: Your full name, address, phone number, and email.
  • Good Faith Statement: A statement that you believe the use is not authorized by the owner or the law.

3. Counter-Notification

If your content was removed by mistake, you may file a counter-notice. You must provide your contact details and consent to the jurisdiction of the Courts in Nepal for any legal proceedings related to the material.

4. Repeat Infringers

In accordance with professional platform standards, Skillsync maintains a "Strike Policy." We reserve the right to permanently terminate accounts that repeatedly violate intellectual property rights.

Kathmandu, Nepal

6. Legal Liability

Warning: Submitting a false or malicious copyright claim is a violation of the law in Nepal and may result in liability for damages and legal fees.
