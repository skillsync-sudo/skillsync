Skillsync Privacy Policy

Effective Date: [November 20, 2025]

At Skillsync ("we," "us," or "our"), we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our website, mobile applications, and related services (collectively, the "Services"). This policy complies with Nepal's Electronic Transactions Act, 2063 (2008), and emerging data protection principles, including those outlined in the draft Personal Data Protection Bill. By using Skillsync, you consent to the practices described herein.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide and improve our Services. This includes:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and profile details you provide during registration or sign-in (e.g., via Google Sign-In).
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the Services, such as pages visited, job searches, and internship applications.
  • Content You Provide: Job postings, skill insights, reviews, and other user-generated content.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, and cookies (see our Cookie Policy for details).

We collect this data directly from you or through third-party integrations like Google.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage your account and provide personalized Services.
  • To connect you with internships, jobs, and skill development opportunities.
  • To facilitate job postings and community interactions.
  • To improve our platform, analyze usage, and develop new features.
  • To communicate with you about updates, promotions, or support.
  • To comply with legal obligations under Nepali law.

We process data based on your consent, legitimate interests, or legal requirements, in line with data protection principles.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share it in the following ways:

  • With Employers: To match you with job or internship opportunities based on your profile.
  • With Service Providers: Third-party vendors who help us operate the Services (e.g., hosting or analytics), bound by confidentiality agreements.
  • For Legal Reasons: If required by law, to enforce our terms, or to protect our rights, in compliance with Nepal's laws.
  • Business Transfers: In case of a merger or sale, your data may be transferred.

We ensure all sharing is necessary and lawful.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information, including encryption, access controls, and regular audits. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We are not liable for unauthorized access due to factors beyond our control.

5. Your Rights

Under Nepal's Electronic Transactions Act and emerging data protection frameworks, you have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information.
  • Withdraw consent for data processing where applicable.
  • Object to certain uses of your data.
  • Request data portability.

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@skillsync.com. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to enhance your experience. For details, see our Cookie Policy. You can manage cookies through your browser settings.

7. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside Nepal. We ensure transfers comply with applicable laws and use safeguards like standard contractual clauses.

8. Children's Privacy

Our Services are not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors without parental consent.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via email or our website. Continued use of the Services after changes constitutes acceptance.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at support@skillsync.com or visit our contact page.

Thank you for trusting Skillsync with your information. We are dedicated to safeguarding your privacy.
