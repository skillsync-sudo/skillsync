Cookie Policy

Last Updated: November 20, 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how Skillsync uses cookies and similar technologies. By continuing to browse our platform, you consent to our use of cookies in accordance with Nepal's Electronic Transactions Act, 2063 and privacy best practices.

1. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device. They act as a memory for the website, allowing Skillsync to remember your device and preferences (like login details and language) over time.

2. How We Use Cookies

Type Purpose
Essential Required for login, security, and core site features.
Performance Helps us understand how users interact with Skillsync (Analytics).
Functional Remembers your job search filters and personalized settings.
Advertising Shows relevant internship and course recommendations.

3. Third-Party Integrations

We use trusted partners like Google for analytics and authentication. These partners may set their own cookies to enable features like Google Sign-In. We recommend reviewing their respective privacy policies.

4. Managing Your Preferences

Take Control: Most web browsers allow you to decline cookies through their settings.
  • To opt-out of Google Analytics, visit the Google Opt-out page.
  • Note: Disabling cookies may prevent certain features of Skillsync from working correctly.

5. Compliance with Nepal Law

Under the Individual Privacy Act, 2075, you have the right to know how your digital footprint is tracked. Skillsync ensures that cookie data is used solely for service improvement and is never sold to unauthorized third parties.
